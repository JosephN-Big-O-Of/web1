The History of the Web

The Internet is, in the present day, a very commonly present library of links available at your fingertips. Some might even say it's a necessity. But how did we get here?

The Past

It all started in the 1950s, where computers with barely any memory were taking up whole rooms. Soon enough, progress took over and we were already having proper compilers, programs and the likes in the 60s. ARPANET was the first successful networking project and in 1969 the first message was typed and sent.

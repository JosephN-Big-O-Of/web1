Mobile-First Development: Meaning & Principles

What is it?

Mobile-First Development ensures compatibility of websites with small screens such as smartphones and tablets, and provides equal user experience (UX) to all users. Proposed in 2009 by Luke Wroblewski, it emphasizes designing for mobile before desktop.

Principles & How to Apply It

Key principles of Mobile-First Development include:

  • Keep it simple
  • Visual hierarchy for clarity
  • Use fewer effects and lightweight images
  • Design text for small screens first

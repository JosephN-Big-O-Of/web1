Why and how to stay secure on the internet?

Why is it harmful?

We use the internet in our daily life. Everyone does. Your family, friends... It connects us with the whole world and with one simple mistake, our lives could be ruined. Such dangers include:

  • DoS/DDoS attacks - Flooding servers with traffic
  • Ransomware, Malware and viruses - Can lock files and steal your data to, for example extort money from you
  • Data Breaches - Users' data gets exposed due to outdated systems like the Sony data breach in 2011
  • Fake websites and phishing emails - Scammers use various tactics to grab your attention
  • Cyberbullying
  • Sketchy links and giveaways

Practices to stay secure on the Internet

For Website Owners/Devs

Best Practices for Owners
  1. Regular Security Testing - like pen testing
  2. Keep your softwares updated
  3. Use Web App Firewalls
  4. Encrypt Users' Data - and always monitor and keep log activity

For users

Best Practices for Users
  1. Use Strong Passwords - long, complex ones
  2. Enable 2FA/MFA when possible to minimize risk
  3. Be wary on public WIFI
  4. Only access websites with HTTPS if possible
  5. ALWAYS back up your data!
